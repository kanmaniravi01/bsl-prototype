Quarter summary

Q1 2026 saw net-positive sentiment recovery after the December app outage, driven primarily by the new loyalty tier and the "Reserve Roastery NY" reopening. Total mention volume grew 14% quarter-over-quarter, but share-of-voice dipped 2 points as Dunkin' and Tim Hortons amplified value-pricing messaging in February.

The single biggest negative driver remains the mobile app order-fulfilment theme (−0.41 sentiment, +62% volume). Two distinct outage windows (Jan 18, Mar 04) accounted for 38% of negative mentions in the quarter. ORM response time improved from 14h to 4.2h median.

Recommended focus for Q2: (1) ship the order-status push-notification fix scheduled for May, (2) lean into Reserve / specialty coverage where competitor SoV is rising fastest, (3) prepare crisis playbook for the seasonal union vote in late June.
