Common questions

What happens at the end of the trial?

Your data stays. We email you ten days before the trial ends. No card means no surprise charge.

Can I bring my own API keys?

Yes — for X, Reddit, YouTube, News, Instagram and the LLM provider. Brisk-managed pulls are the default; rotate to your own keys any time from Integrations.

What if I go over my mention quota?

Soft-cap by default — Brisk keeps collecting and adds the overage to your next invoice at $0.01 per mention. Switch to hard-cap from Settings → Usage if you'd rather block.
